###gunicorn部署Flask  
```shell
gunicorn -w 4 -b 0.0.0.0:5000 tinysite:app
```  

###关闭gunicorn
```shell
pstree -ap|grep gunicorn
```
获取gunicorn的进程树，树根为网站进程
```shell
kill -9 树根进程
```
退出任务
